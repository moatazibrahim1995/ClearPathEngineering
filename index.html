Costly mistakes without expert guardrails

  • No draft, uneven walls (IM): sinks/warpage → re‑tooling & weeks lost.
  • Undercuts by accident: surprise side‑actions inflate tool cost.
  • Over‑tight tolerances: quotes spike; parts still don’t fit.
  • No datum scheme: vendors can’t inspect; quality ping‑pong.
  • Part count bloat: assembly takes forever; redesign late.
  • No supplier engaged: RFQ cycles stall; wrong assumptions linger.
  • Certs ignored (UL/FCC/CE): late test failures → redesign.

How we prevent waste

  • Early DFM + tolerance pass on priority parts
  • Deliberate parting lines, draft, radii review
  • Right‑sized tolerances & finishes for cost/fit
  • DFA: reduce part count and fastener chaos
  • Supplier‑ready RFQ pack; 2–3 quotes fast
  • Regulatory pre‑check to avoid late surprises

Time saved = months. Money saved = avoiding re‑tool, scrap, and quote shock.
